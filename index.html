Human-AI Interaction

We work on interactive machine learning techniques, immersive and visual analytics, and AI-driven computer user interface methods that promote mutual understanding and cooperation between humans and AI. For this to succeed, both humans and AI must be able to communicate with each other, provide feedback, and act on that knowledge. The result for us is the use of an AI that enables fluid cooperation between humans and machines and gives us the freedom to use our human strength of genuine creativity.

Our Research Foci

Mixed initiative interaction

Human-in-the-loop methods (interfaces, models and systems) are developed that enable people to obtain information about AI decisions and to give feedback. The AI will be able to gather the human input to improve the performance of the human-machine team.

Explainable AI

The aim is to develop explainable algorithms and to offer the user methods for interpreting the learning processes and reasoning of the AI. Explainable AI plays a key role in AI trustworthiness and acceptance. Research focuses on algorithms for calculating explainable features, methods for validating and analysing them, and methods for explaining these features in an understandable form.

Human-aware AI models

Our goal is to develop intelligent systems that can "reflect" their actions in relation to human perception, intentions and other contextual factors. We are interested in collecting data and generating data-driven models to understand what the affective and emotional state of people in different interactions with technology looks like and how it influences the interaction. Such systems are designed to adapt to different human states while creating models of human intentions and behaviors.

Data Driven Immersive Analytics

Motivation

The considerable sums being invested in digitization are leading to complex “cyberphysical systems”. Still, despite recent advances, the sensory experience offered by immersive technologies is rather limited. Data is available, but access, interaction, and analysis are separate from the physical entity. These are tedious activities when done in the physical world. Visualization and interaction metaphors and analysis methods were simply not developed to represent a physical world. So, how should information about physically and digitally existing entities best be combined in a coherent presentation?

Objectives

The goal is to provide personalized experiences with data and analytics anchored in the real world – for the individual participant or together with peers locally or remotely. We do so by:
i- Exploring models of embodied interaction with immersive analytics.
ii- Developing paradigms for collaborative immersive remote analysis.
iii- Exploring personalization based on physiological sensing and social immersive training.
In a nutshell, we investigate how to present and interact with immersive data views, what physiological features lead to perceptual optimization and personalization, and how to use traces of interactions in the environment to create recommendations and guidance.

Foundation AI that learns with humans

A holistic approach to your own AI

In the rapidly evolving landscape of technology, businesses are increasingly recognizing the importance of integrating Artificial Intelligence into their operations. With the overarching goal of aligning AI models with specific business requirements, we set forth comprehensive objectives and a strategic approach to create a robust framework for the deployment and development of our own AI models.

Objectives

The primary objective of our initiative is to establish a framework that facilitates the seamless deployment of AI models, ensuring they are in harmony with the unique requirements of your business. This involves a meticulous process of research and development to not only create AI models but also to continually enhance their learning capabilities while retaining past knowledge. The dynamic nature of the field necessitates a proactive approach to research and development, which includes methods to interactively steer the AI based on evolving circumstances.

Approach

Our approach centers on collaborative co-creation, emphasizing close cooperation with stakeholders to deeply understand their challenges. By actively involving them in the development process, we tailor AI models to meet specific business requirements, enhancing their relevance and fostering stakeholder ownership. A key aspect involves developing finely tuned solutions that align with unique business needs through thorough research and development. Our goal is not just technological deployment but creating AI solutions that bring tangible value and efficiency to operations. Additionally, we are committed to incorporating human agency and responsible AI practices into our core development process by embedding essential values and ethics. To enhance transparency and accountability, we offer explainable interactive techniques, enabling users to understand and steer the AI's decision-making processes.
